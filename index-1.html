About Us

Adfield is a firm that was built with a passion to improve the outdoor advertising standards in India. It was founded in 1998 By Lokesh Rao and enterprising outdoor professionals led by Abhishek L Rao, Adfield is the AOR (Agency on Record) for media planning and buying for many leading companies and advertisers in Mysore, Our clientele ranges from large out-of-home spenders to even medium spenders, but each one having recognizable and respected brands. We don't believe in 'one size fits all' solutions. That is why we manage to create campaigns that are successful even with medium to small budgets, and have also succeeded in reaching to target groups who have low exposure in OOH. We dare to challenge the convention and place quality at the center of every piece of work we do.
